Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, an iconic Italian dish, features layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The preparation involves boiled lasagna noodles, a flavorful meat sauce with aromatic herbs, and a luscious béchamel sauce. These components are layered with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese before baking to golden perfection. The result is a comforting and hearty dish, cherished globally for its harmonious blend of textures and flavor

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Whole milk
  • Mozzarella cheese(shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese(grated)
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Lasagna Noodles
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce
  3. Make Bechamel Sauce
  4. Assemble Layers
  5. Top With Cheese
  6. Bake
  7. Rest Before Serving
  8. Serve
